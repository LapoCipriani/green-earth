About us

Mission

GreenEarth works to restore ecosystems and protect the oceans. We commit to concrete reforestation and ocean cleanup projects, combining innovation, community, and technology to create real and lasting impact.

Projects

Every action counts. GreenEarth turns ideas into tangible results: from planting trees to collecting ocean waste, each project is guided by real impact and sustainability.

Our Projects

Project 1: Urban Reforestation

We transform abandoned urban areas into city forests, improving air quality and creating green spaces accessible to everyone.

Project 2: Ocean Cleanup

Millions of tons of plastic enter the oceans every year. Through volunteer campaigns and innovative technologies, we remove waste and raise community awareness.

Project 3: Environmental Education

We educate new generations about the value of nature, offering courses, workshops, and hands-on labs for schools and communities.
